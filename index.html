About Me

I am a licensed professional counselor. I have been working for South Texas Neuropsychology since 2004. I hold an undergraduate degree from Baylor University in Communication Disorders and a Graduate Degree from The University of Texas of San Antonio in Counseling. Well versed in rehabilitation, neurological disorders, and brain injuries during my internship at HealthSouth Rehabilitation Institute of San Antonio (RIOSA), where I began providing counseling and psychometric services. I have run traumatic brain injury and stroke counseling as well as led support groups at area hospitals. I also participated as a sub-investigator in clinical trials exploring the effects of interventions on various cognitive and neurological disorders.
